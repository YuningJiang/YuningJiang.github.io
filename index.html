Welcome to Yuning's homepage! I am currently a research staff at School of Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My supervisor is Nanyang Assistant Professor, Junsong Yuan. I also work closely with Prof. Jiebo Luo from University of Rochester, NY, USA.

My research topic is on computer vision and multimedia. Specfically, my current interests cover on visual object search, image retrieval and natural scene recoginition.

This homepage will provide you with more infomation about me, so you can get a close look at me. :)
